FROM THE EDITOR'S DESK

The month of February was wonderful. We marked two anniversaries and some of us voted for the very first time. No unfortunate incidences occured during the month and we can only be grateful to God for all of this and more. So, as we begin a new month, it is my prayer that the month of March would be even better and more rewarding. Amen.

An African adage says: "if you want to go fast, walk alone but if you want to go far, walk together". Life is more of a marathon than a sprint and as such, the better approach should be going far instead of fast. Finding a team for this journey is even easier for us since we have one another. Our individual goals should be much easier to achieve when we work as a group with eyes on our collective goal - a family to be reckoned with. For those of us who can still recall way back in 2020 when we agreed to start pulling funds together, we were able to get a new van for dad within the first quarter of 2021. That was one of the first proofs of what we can achieve as a united group. We continued forging on and even came up with a name for our own company, VERiNOM. It may have been out of an initial burst of enthusiasm which to be honest has really watered down because barely two years on, a lot of us have relented and some of us are even yet to join the train.

However, I am excited to state here that the vision is not dead but it will take sincere efforts from all of us to reignite that enthusiasm. Who says we can't own our own company together? I know we all have dreams of our own but the idea of the VERiNOM group in itself is to marry all of our dreams into one. The accomplishment of every individual dream would mean a step forward in accomplishing the VERiNOM dream. So, let's not hold back in working together as a team. Let every step we take be for the collective growth of this family. We can be more than we currently are. I believe.

So, to a much better approach from all of us, I give a hearty cheer! God bless us all. Amen.

THE ORIGIN OF THE PISCES

March was originally called Martius and used to be the first month of the earliest Roman calendar. The month is named after the Roman god of war, Mars. The month of March symbolically represents new growth, fertility, and procreation. This is because March is the time of the year when animals start waking up from hibernation and also because it is the beginning of Spring.

DID YOU KNOW?

The oldest-known living land animal is a tortoise named Jonathan, which is about to turn 190 years old. He was born in 1832 and has lived on the Island of St Helena in the Atlantic Ocean since 1882.

Also, the oldest person ever to have lived (whose age could be authenticated), a French woman named Jeanne Louise Calment, was 122 years old when she died in 1997.

HEALTH AND LIFESTYLE

FEED YOUR GUT BACTERIA

The bacteria in you gut, collectively called the gut microbiota, are incredibly important for overall health. A disruption in gut bacteria is linked to some chronic diseases, including obesity and a myriad of digestive problems.

Good ways to improve gut health include eating probiotic foods like yogurt and sauerkraut, taking probiotic supplements when indicated, and eating plenty of fiber. Notably, fiber serves as a prebiotic or a food source for your gut bacteria. (healthline.com, 2023).
