THE ORIGIN OF THE CAPRICORNS

January was the last month to have been added to the calendar, which originally had ten months and began in March. During his reign (c. 715-673), Numa revised the Roman calendar so that January replaced March as the first month of the year. It was deemed a fitting choice, since January was named after Janus - the Roman god of all beginnings and endings, who presided over doors and gates.

Janus is usually depicted with two faces - one looking forward and the other looking backward.

And in the year 1622, the Catholic church adopted January 1 as the beginning of the year instead of March 25.

DID YOU KNOW

Did you know that dentistry is probably the oldest profession in the world?

Come to think of it: "humans have always had teeth" and if that is true, then we have always taken care of our teeth in one way or another! Isn't that what dentistry is all about?

Okay, but on a more serious note, one study found evidence of teeth being drilled in skulls that date from 7,500 to 9,000 years ago. The holes were likely made using a prehistoric bow drill. Another research conducted by the University of Bologna, Italy, on a 14,000 year old skull found that one rotten tooth in the jaw had been deliberately scoured and scraped with a tool. These research findings make dentistry one of the oldest recorded professions in human history!

THE JANUARY TITBIT

Persistence is one attribute that is common amongst successful people. Many people want to get to the top but only a handfull of people actually get there. Many a time, these are the people who didn't give up. They kept going despite the odds being against them most of the time.

Our generation can find excuses for anything and everything but the truth remains: to achieve anything, you have to be persistent at it. So, after setting that goal, it is on you to cultivate the persistent attitude to ensure that you achieve it. The goal could be a financial target. You may have said that by the end of this year, you want to have half a million in your account. It could be an academic target to finish school. It could be to learn a skill. Whatever it is, most times, it is only achievable when you decide to keep going no matter what. It is up to you to not give up.

Also, be humble. Be teachable. Be ready to learn. People are where they are today because they put in the work. Learn from them. They've seen a lot of what you are just seeing. Experience is the best teacher and they have experience.
